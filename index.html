Publications CV

Hello! I am Varun, a pre-doctoral researcher at Google DeepMind, India. I am fortunate to be working with brilliant researchers in Maching Learning Optimizations team led by Dr. Prateek Jain. I am broadly interested in making current language models reason better with fundamental solutions.

Over the past few years, I have worked on a variety of problems as a budding researcher. Most recently, I worked on extracting unsupervised feedback from text to improve LLMs at various tasks with Dr. Karthikeyan Shanmugam and Dr. Arun Suggala. I also worked on speeding up LLM inference with Dr. Praneeth Netrapalli. Before that, I collaborated with Prof. Vivek Gupta on supplementing LLMs with knowledge graphs and testing reasoning abilities of Visual LLMs. I also spent a summer working at Amazon Science, Bangalore on modeling customer demographics through their behavioral history.

My interest in machine learning and its applications stems from my relentless participation in hackathons and challenges. Along with numerous kaggle wins, my team also placed second in Amazon Machine learning challenge 2021. After working on diverse and interesting problems real world problems, my curious self pursued the path of research to dive deeper into fundamental aspects of machine learning and language.
